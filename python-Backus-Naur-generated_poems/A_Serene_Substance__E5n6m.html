A Serene Substance


The I in magnolium you say, what is the eloquence of lawyers waiting for in its green trouser?
I tell you it is waiting for doctrine of doctors like you.
A bicycle
is not enough to kill me and keep me
from the city of your affluent epiphany.

Towards those moons of yours that wait for me.
Return to the homeland of the sea shells.


A free sunshine of fleets of cars.
Everything insatiable with esoteric voices, the salt of the skein of wool
and piles of romantic bread behind early light of day.
