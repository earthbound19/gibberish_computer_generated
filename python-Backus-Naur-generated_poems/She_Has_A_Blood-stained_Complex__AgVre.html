She Has A Blood-stained Complex


I was without doubt the cousin cat
there in the frail jungle.
When it looked me with its human flower head eyes
it had neither armpit nor shoulder
but cedar disguisings of tailors on its sides.
A current of dashing root
that does not know why it flows and protects.
I stayed continued and transparent
among the land.
Be guided by the hidden stalks of cattail's grace.
