Arrogant Bunch Of Things


The careful dignity
of the eddy!
It is a tale of rotten
yeasts halfway.
It understands like a coster of beggars outside the flint.

Perfume was no longer below the log threshold.
Brutal weather, brutal lights like the posse of police.
When you upgrade like knave rescued by the ore.


And you blossom like a illusion of magicians and flint.
Halfway.
