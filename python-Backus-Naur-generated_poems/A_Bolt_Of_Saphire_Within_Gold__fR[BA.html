A Bolt Of Saphire Within Gold


Your drop is a leaf filled with morose damning of jurors.
Not to tread or even meet
the thread of one who perfumes
in me in a chimney or perfuming to a stranger.

Which is a cordial fleet of ships of directions
twelve or million, enriched
on a breakfast or in the cordial bird feather
directions of the finger, a calculation in your breaths.
I crystallize as if in a hated shards of silicon.
Sometimes a piece of the plasma
brainwashes like a shoreline in my finger.
But the baptism of water kissed the memory.
The cleansed goddess
shines in the lovely morning.
