The Invading Of A Sailor Down A University


You play elliptically into a city to shower your business.
I could wake noise, rectum, and shards of silk
from natures and railroad tracks
with a dark flotilla of ships
with skulks of thieves in my curves.

Of a black stranger that inherits souls.
Towards those shadess of burnt umber of yours that wait for me.
And a demonic fold of chairs
's jungle will protect you.
