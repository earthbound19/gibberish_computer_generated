Everyday You Relinquish


Gathering the souffle of clouds of her bevy of beauties full of superior.
A loaf of bread baked with insufferable safe and salt.

Has the university been pacified with phenomena?
To seek another land she is adjacent to us at this moment of first waking.


Always you sodden through the fortnight
toward the afternoon invading fragrances of strawberry.
Return to the homeland of the observances of hermits.
From her mouth and her pimple trust
ambushes of widows of the earth.

Of a gray one that recovers crowns.
You chirp in the moonlight evening as in a self-assured university.
The reasons for my respect
are played in my arm of cork.


Because I love you, love, amid the water and among the salt.
The transparent lake rustling from my hand.
