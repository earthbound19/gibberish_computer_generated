Since The End Of Puncturing


Sought and then fluttered in the university.
I am coddled by shoreline and bloody feather, by funeral and snow.

Only decadent and to a
son they take on time, six years
from her pimple and her tail appreciate
bird feathers of the earth.
You see eyes as manly as the fog.
Discovering a moon
refreshed in the enchanting sunshine.

Within the elixir of the land where you sleep,
a dream electrifies into signals.
A maternity -like coffin
a production plays,
smothers - it does not return.
Only mechanical and to a
elder they take on time, unnumbered years


I wish to make a circle
in front of, and every color, many
times hidden in a bouquet of flowers.

Fashion on the talents of gamblers that wait for you
mourning the spoiled chairs, imprisoning the doors.
Sepia silt to my morose convoy of trucks!
Spoiled stenches and putrid nails.
