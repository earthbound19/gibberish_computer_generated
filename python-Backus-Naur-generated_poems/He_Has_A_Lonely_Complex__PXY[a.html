He Has A Lonely Complex


A flute fashioning will flutter
the inevitable salt of a planet.
The friendship plan that has everyone motionless.
Because I love you, love, next to the copper and outside the gypsum.

A phenomenon preserves,
drops - it does not return.
Not the crimson moment
when the night weaves the necklaces.
Like nails smothering behind peals of bells.


Shall we move on?
The ore fluidic alarms are killed.
