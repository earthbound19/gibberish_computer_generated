Brides Of A Mechanical Country


Multitude of troupes of dancers!
In and out of the silvery the cashmire and the sunburst orange a tangent arc amid a quadrangle,
the rustling workings of boundless law.
I took on browbeaten phemonanas.

A raft is not enough to undulate me and keep me
from the room of your brandishing curiosities.
The furious nature is comfortable on your fingernails.
