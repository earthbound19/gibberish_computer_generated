Tell Him That I Am Giving Up Seeking In Productions


You perch my calculating granule
like a humble lobster to fresh plum.
Wave of wave of branches rolling down the sea.

This listless bevy of beauties and relinquishing prize forebodes me
with it's serene companies of soldiers like mouth and loins
and red lemons like tail and honeysuckles.
And worships of writers and rivers.


Nothing but your cosmic palm.
