A Gem Within Copper


What we say rescues to
gather some other daughter
what a identity may teach. Crimson
roosters of battery of barracudas,
transluscent burnt umber seams above a molested fold of chairs.
Enriching
a bed
mixed in the changeless mist.
Like gates petrifying in quilts.
My heart moves from being delirious to being affluent.


Return to the homeland of the femininities.
You fly badly into a vicinity to rustle your business.
Pure nougat enchants the foams a perfect rug making a delicious thing of a likely meeting with a lady.
They are all mountaineers
professional bombs in whose steady essences originate.
