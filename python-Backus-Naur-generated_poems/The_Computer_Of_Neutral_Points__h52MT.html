The Computer Of Neutral Points


Parched fortnight and the shifty quiver
loiter at the walls of my house.
Where hats meet
sun rises meet, in and amid and the sound
of oxides, to reach out and seize in needy.
Enjoy the many rotten attempts to chirp
the angellic havoc.
There is natural fortune in travelling it.

Mingle on the clandenstines that wait for you
disguising the raucous chairs, degrading the doors.
