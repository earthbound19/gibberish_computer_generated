A Clear Substance


A circle among a tangent arc,
the guilt workings of plumed law.
Enriching from nauesous ceramic.

Enjoy the many harsh attempts to store
the cordial stalactite.
There is fleeting fortune in rising it.
You've asked me what the bird is gathering there with his marine loins?
I reply, the sea water knows this.

I do not overflow in the area of melancholy cadaver.
