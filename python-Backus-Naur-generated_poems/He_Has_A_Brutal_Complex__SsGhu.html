He Has A Brutal Complex


Nobody here is waiting for the next posse of police.
Bevy of ladies. You formed yourself for lighting. A circle behind a line,
the oily workings of cosmic law.
Draw from it the wet-winged
technique of its own detail.

You say, what is the chorus of singers waiting for in its cinnamon troupe of dancers?
I tell you it is waiting for flower head like you.
