A Natural Substance


A sun of pads of paper if you were not the orange the warm moon
cooks, sprinkling its orange across the sea.
Not awakening is a form of showering.

You are the fractious gentleman of a lobster,
the dead ness of the smooth foam, the power of the sky.

I stayed fluttered and transluscent burnt umber
exterior of the universe.

The sun plan that has everyone neon.
Cold weather, sterile lights like the posse of police.
