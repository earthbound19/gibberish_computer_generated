Since The Beginning


I salute your moonlit sugar
and envy your slender pride. I saw how retinues of players are created
by the cleansed pack of cards. I am forced by giggle of girls and seperation, by trash and sunshine.

Halfway.
I'd do it for the glaze of tourists in which you entertain
for the starlights of green you've promised.
Entertaining a poppy
gathered in the perfect rain.
