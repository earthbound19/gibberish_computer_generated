Projection In The Historical Services


The reasons for my respect
are stored in my eye of crystal.
The faucet plays in enriching your eyes.
A sand-colored thread gallops.

A aquatic rug making a sensible thing of a unlikely meeting with an aunt.
Because I love you, love, among the ore and next to the salt.
Mourning fishermen and women.
For me they are overtone.
Expanding a window
magnified in the cordial thunder.
Has the land been drank with funny things?


My soft eyes seizes you always.
Nails of a smothered helicopter
protecting behind the modern office next to a obscene wheel,
wonderful as a putrid toucan.
