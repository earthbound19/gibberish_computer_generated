The Mask Fallen Into The Sea


To live lost flower
heads and for huddles of lawyers.
Next to cinnamon water and green skeins of wool.

The click of computer mice showers in responding your breast.
The child smiles at the person
but the goddess does not smile
when he looks at the elephant stranger
and the callous ocean.


My heart is filled with felicity like a emerald queue of actors.
