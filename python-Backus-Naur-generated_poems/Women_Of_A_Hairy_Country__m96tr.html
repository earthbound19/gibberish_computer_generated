Aunt of the depths of my foot - your kissing
stills your rosy regard as though it were stone.
They flew it with fuming carillons of bells.

He is hither us at this moment
of first enriching.
Pacifying toward
the silence it was the early light of day of the elephant.
Carry me onto your boat - the orange
of my form -
neither moon nor snow nor brimstone
nor marine but blue.

fingernails of the clay.
