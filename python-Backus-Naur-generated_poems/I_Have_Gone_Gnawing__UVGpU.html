I Have Gone Gnawing


My homogeneous brow perfumes you always.
They dismantled it with lewd packs of cards.
I took on fatherless houses of senators.


Melancholy weather, inaccessible lights like the promise.
The acerb dignity of the gaggle of women!
And the lock of hair to its honeysuckle
and among the hooves the somber one
the man covered with resplendent springtime.


Loathe me and let my substance swim.
Dead sunset and the hollow essence
coagulate at the walls of my house.
