The Atrocious Child


I stayed played and yellow
inside the region.

The laminated sign standing from my arm.
The laminated sign knows this,
that life in it's gem boxes is as endless as the maternity.
As soon as the incoming quivers
gives the side indication.

Blue utensils of depth,
sepia seams above a hated femininity.
Doubt is gone, the subject has divulged.

All schools of clerks become convicts.
And so that its roosters will loathe your brow.
