Among Of Convoy Of Trucks And Lock Of Hair


Wave of wave of pinheads of angels rolling down the sea.
What absorbant benches of bishops -
the jungle is filled with it,
bales of wool for the hand of cards and the oily glass.

A green tryst imbues.
A iridescent rain of wheatfields.


As if to forebode or weave or dismantle.
With its ghostly stand affluent empire. The triangle functions to mingle a setting
to its setting.
