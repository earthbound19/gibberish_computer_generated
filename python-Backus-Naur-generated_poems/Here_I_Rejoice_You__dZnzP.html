Here I Rejoice You


Molested hearts and distorted bones.
I discover as if outside a rusted depth.
But the rascal of boys woke the memory.
Began and then recovered in the field.

Carry me onto your raft - the apple of my muster of soldiers -
halfway.
You are going to ask where are the fill?
And the pure pastures?
And the mist self-assured splattering its graces and harassing them full of
jungle and bird?
