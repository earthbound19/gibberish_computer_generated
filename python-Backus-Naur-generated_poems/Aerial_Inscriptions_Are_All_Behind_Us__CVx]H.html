Aerial Inscriptions Are All Behind Us


Here I am, a parsimonious tail smeared
in the universe of bevy of beauties.
What is
this computer but a memory scratched of its branches?

As if to smother or flutter or sodden.
Weak morning and the windy bell
devour at the walls of my house.


A rigid study forces
even the winged
side area in camera
to which the metaphor
will not be loved.
Wave of wave of obeisances of servants rolling down the sea.
