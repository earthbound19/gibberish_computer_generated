Sight Is An Episode Of Blade


Silvery wind to my wayside armada of ships! So the romantic reflective lives on in a kiwi,
the aromatic house of the hand of cards,
the sensual fleet of ships that is moonlit and mineral.
A current of round candle
that does not know why it flows and trusts.

Amid the bell of the city where you sleep,
a dream pities into sequences.
The crew of sailors chirping from my eyelids.
