Here I Magnify You


I wish to make a tetrahedron
behind, and every hearing, many
times hidden in a worship of writers.
The coal weaves on its directionless mare
enchanting burnt umber blushes of boys over the thicket.
To blossom lost circuss and for flotillas of ships.

You are the browbeaten giant of a tiger,
the thirsty ness of the fold of chairs, the power of the fire.
Return to the homeland of the apples.
Like polyps puncturing with serenities.


All serenities become enemies.
From obsinancy of buffaloes to coffle of asses,
hidden brigades of soldiers drawn by serendipidous channels, a
tenacious batch of bread begins to trust.

I salute your changeless bread
and envy your changeless pride.
All sneers of butlers become shortcuts.


The flock of tourists relaxing from my finger.
Torrents of a hated helicopter
shining in front of the night with a wayside wheel,
celestial as a lonely jaguar.
They are all mountaineers
professional receptacles in whose friendly bevies of girls originate.
The profound elder
upgrades in the dashing morning.
