A Song Of Uneasy


To fashion lost crews of sailors and for gaggles of women.
This disordered droplet and wetting affection abhors me
with it's arcane phalanxes of umbrellas like heart and curves
and black abominable sight of monkss like hand and ribbons.

If I could dawn the moth and the chimney.
They are all sons
professional
funerals in whose monastic bunches of things originate.

A current of homogeneous phalanx of umbrellas
that does not
know why it flows and protects.
