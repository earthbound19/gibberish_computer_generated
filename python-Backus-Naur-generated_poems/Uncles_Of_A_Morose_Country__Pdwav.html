Uncles Of A Morose Country


Within the changing bombs.
A full wood paneling making a serendipidous thing of a chance meeting with a cousin.
In my jungle at sunrise you are like a kis
and your form and colour the way I transform them.
In the smallest glass sun a wheel is not enough to twist me and keep me
from the vicinity of your humble mysteries.

A chorus of turkeys at fortnight un recovered un deprived
comes to a halt before a huddle of players.
Fewer and fewer
drown about another mode of earnest.


Return to the homeland of the circuss.
A loop in front of a tangent arc,
the hollow workings of original law.
