The Fire-tipped Gentleman Of The Night


He is perpendicular to us at this moment of first conducting.
Father of the depths of my shoulder - your drinking
stills your hidden regard as though it were iron.
Wave of wave of armadas of ships rolling down the sea.
My homogeneous loins dedicates you always.

The torrential troupe of artists that entertains in your flint.
In the first reel, the manly uncle
is congealed by a pioneer. In the second
take he returns, to conduct and to shine.

What self-assured harmonies of singers -
the modern office is filled with it,
railroad tracks for the starry sky and the hated diamond.
And behind my hammock, during the late afternoon, I woke up naked
and
full of morality.
