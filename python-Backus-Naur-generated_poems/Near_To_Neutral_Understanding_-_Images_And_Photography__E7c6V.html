Near To Neutral Understanding - Images And Photography


The mosaic conducting from my brain.
It is a tale of absurd vigils with sepia water and cashmire companies of actors.
A current of full carillon of bells
that does not know why it flows and transforms.
Not the burnt umber moment
when the sunrise swims the drops.

Our new impatience of wives, our lovely queue of actors tetrahedrons.
There ought to be a fellowship of a resplendent lunar wetting in a city.
A arrogant metaphor replaces
even the winged
minor moonlight evening in signal
to which the metaphor
will not be drank.

A study relinquishes,
deludes - it does not
return.
What seems simultaneous to one will not seem so to another.
Sometimes a piece of the aluminum
strikes like a blush of boys in my chest.


What overflows the props of kindness?
Has the jungle been shone with secrets?

Shaken evils and wounded smokes.
Pockets of brush converted into ivory.
All necklaces become cities.
Like depths killing next to evening stars.

I salute your vertical bread
and envy your slender pride.
Like tenacious paper-mache architecture, scoldings of seamstresses
not the marine moment
when the fortnight swims the trees.
Pockets of rusted nail converted into silicon.
