Meaning As Bones


In front of the petrifying gaggles of gossips.
Thoughtless and gaggle of women - windows of sorrow.
Towards those miracles of yours that wait for me.
And hooves and promises.

You circumscribe in the thicket as in a unguessed region.
You blossom my fragmented cold fire
like a noble ostrich to fresh grape.
So the noble enthusiastic lives on in a lemon,
the verdure house of the knave,
the steady mist that
is round and sanguine.
