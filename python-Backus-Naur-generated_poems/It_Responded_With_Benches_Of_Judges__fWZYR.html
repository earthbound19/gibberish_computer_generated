It Responded With Benches Of Judges


Inside the bitter moonlight evening of imperalist silken architecture.
Towards those convoys of trucks of yours that wait for me.

The I in flesh I salute your esoteric plum
and
envy your aquatic pride. A misunderstood bale of wool day
and you'll ask why doesn't his poetry
entertain of writings and bevies of beauties
and the homogeneous bird feathers of his native land?
