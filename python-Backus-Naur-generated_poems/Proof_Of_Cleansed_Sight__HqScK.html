Proof Of Cleansed Sight


If you were not the apple the eloquent moon
cooks, sprinkling its nectarine across the region.
What seems simultaneous to one will not seem so to another.

What we say refreshes to
preserve some other son
what a detail may teach.
What bristles the props of bold?


The absorbant dignity of the time!
And so that its deaths will congeal your shoulder.
