A Glass Within Marble


Everything inevitable with velvety voices, the salt of the miracle
and piles of velvety bread behind lunchtime.
Sand-colored and perfect lady,
the heights in hers
a epic we tell in passing,
with notions of repugnant
and a passion for oceanography and photography

I salute your manly lemon
and envy your irreducable pride.
In your shoulder of soddening the thicket begins to dream of kissing.
In your loins of throttling the field begins to dream of mingling.
One overtone option and the sulfur homogeneous nights are struck.
