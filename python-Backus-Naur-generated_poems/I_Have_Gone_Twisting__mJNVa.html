I Have Gone Twisting


The phemonana faints, the armada of ships of celestial
perches inside.
Jovial is gone, the subject has sought.

Around
the night I like to wake like a disordered crew of sailors.
Carry me onto your vessel - the orange of my company of soldiers -
always you smear through the afternoon
toward the sunrise shaking fleets of cars.
Some dawn but I recover your brush like pencil.


On what senile shadows upgraded with wind?
Of your red team of players when you hold out your brain.
