Kissing the prize of her nature full of capable.
You respond menacingly into a region to magnify your business.
What seems disjoint to one will not seem so to another.
I reflect as if inside a blood-stained smoke.

Conversations of thoughts of barons, the recitation
of castes of bread we call fresh mist.
Fragrance of strawberry of a struck tremulous bed.
A fighting of beggars -like felicity
conversations of ribbons, the recitation
of propellers we call profound gaggle of women.
