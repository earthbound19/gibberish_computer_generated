Meeting Your Coven Of Witches


Only prize, just the
love, nothing but
it. Foliage.
To re-cover lost saxophones and for decks of sailors.
Began and then rose in the university.
I took on oily seas of bishops.

Shall we continue?
Under the field like ash.
The frightened jaguar flutters
apex of the sweet-smelling flames.
