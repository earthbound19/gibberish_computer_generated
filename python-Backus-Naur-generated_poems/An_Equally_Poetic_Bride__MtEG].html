An Equally Poetic Bride


In the smallest paper-mache ripple my heart moves
from being insatiable to being homogeneous. I'd do it for the drop in which you play
for the choirs of angels of green you've attracted.
And so that its consequences will crack your eyelids.
Silvery and electrical pioneer,

From her fingernails and her eye hear
candles of the earth.
Where
affections meet
companies of soldiers meet, outside and outside and the sound
of skulks of thieves, to reach out and drink in intolerant.
A current of poetic library of books
that does not know why it flows and loves.


A transparent choir of singers preserves.
Within the opaque rust colored disdainful of the wasteland.

And so that its whispers will taunt your finger.
Closed off and pulled out like a glaze of tourists.
