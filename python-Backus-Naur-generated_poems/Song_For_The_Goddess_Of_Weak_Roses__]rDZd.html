Song For The Goddess Of Weak Roses


You rejoice in the modern office as in a honest chimney.
You - the indespensable breath.

In the face of so many waxes to functionality.
Enjoy the many putrid
attempts to grow
the parsimonious lance.
There is unguessed fortune in mingling it.

Full stop.
What we say divulges to
shine some other uncle
what a synonym may teach.
My heart moves from being whirlwinds of to being round.
My self-assured armpit mingles you always.

I stand as if in front of a pale receptacle.
Enjoy the many burned-out attempts to blossom
the stationary unction of undertakers.
There is brandishing fortune in flowing it.
