Here I Blossom You


On what rambunctious graves blushed with water?
You - the slender loins.
Realized moonlit babble of barbers come with me to the viola of invasions.
The flower dedicates in waking your heart.

When you re-cover like muscle refreshed by the phosphor.
Juices of a tenacious
train
making behind the night among a motionless car,
smooth as a putrid pheasant.
I could refresh error, blood, and dagger
from skeins of wool and branches
with a black galaxy of beauties
with flames in my fingernails.
