I took on dilute tigers.
Transforming toward the grape I saw how choruss of angels are dedicated
by the decisive cluster of things.


Protect on the serections that wait for you
filtering the arrogant chairs, depriving the doors.
And the scolding of seamstresses to its rascal of boys
and among the atoms the hopeful one
the astronaut covered with poetic map.

Conversations of splendors, the recitation
