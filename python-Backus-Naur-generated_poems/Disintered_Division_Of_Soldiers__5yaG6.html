Disintered Division Of Soldiers


There are no vigils but obscene cycles of drop and sunburst orange
choirs of singers of parsimonious tenacious sand.
A loaf of bread baked with rabid jubilant and salt.
The region outside hers
a tale we divulge in passing,
with notions of humility
and a passion for oceanography and engineering

So the sensible hope lives on in a lemon,
the changeless house of the honeysuckle,
the serene obeisance of servants that is wide and cordial.
