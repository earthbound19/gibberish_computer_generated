From What Are Autumns Understood


Conversations of telegraphs, the recitation
of damnings of jurors we call nocturnal quiver of arrows.
A leg and a mouth
standing the night.
A current of celestial peace
that does not know why it flows and circumscribes.
Cinnamon nights of bone,
blue seams above a weak abominable sight of monks.


Imbue on the deaths that wait for you
scratching the chaotic chairs, scratching the doors. Rejoiced and then mingled in the land.
The chimney behind hers
a epic we divulge in passing,
with notions of sullen
and a passion for science and engineering
they are all sons
professional wounded soldiers in whose absorbant banners originate.
I inherit as if behind a lethargic conglomerate.
