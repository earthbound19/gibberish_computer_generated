Since The End


And the foliage to its kis
and among the forms the changeless one
the elder covered with natural candle.
Shall we go forward?

From her foot and her eye mingle
galaxies of beauties of the earth.
So the delicious amazed lives on in a orange,
the electric house of the garden,
the profound bench of judges that is friendly and silent.

And outside my hammock, during the morning, I woke up naked
and full of euphoric.
Rusted gods and pioneers.
For me they are minor.
