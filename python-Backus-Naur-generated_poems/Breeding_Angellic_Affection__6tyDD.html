Breeding Angellic Affection


Always you plague through the day
toward the midnight imprisoning umbrellas.
Your momentum is a phalanx of umbrellas filled with parched library of books.
You are the apple of my rustling fingernails.

And so that its dungs will coddle your eye.
The self-assured dignity of the garden!
A vessel is not enough to forebode me and keep me
from the universe of your secure curiosities.


I stayed dedicated and sand-colored
betwixt the night.
Sometimes a piece of the steel
deceives like a bench of bishops in my arm.

Multitude of acrobats!
In and out of the blue the sepia and the cinnamon
return to the homeland of the networks of computers.


A current of decisive lock of hair
that does not know why it flows and breathes.
