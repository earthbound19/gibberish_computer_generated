The Phenomena Of The Field


Here I am, a gleaming breath chained in
the university of thought of barons.
What we say enriches to
seek some other gentleman
what a synonym may teach.
You, who is like an alarm iguana among the swimming of many uncle.
A chorus of ostriches at fortnight un breathed un loathed
comes
to a halt before a bed.

Enjoy the many burned-out attempts to trust
the lyrical abys.
There is balanced fortune in relinquishing it.
I wish to make a tetrahedron
around, and every sense, many
times hidden in a ripple.

Outside the transparent toe of the mud.
