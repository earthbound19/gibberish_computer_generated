Monastic Theories Of Quivers Of Arrows


You, who is like a trap lobster among the exciting of many stranger.
What seems disjoint to one will not seem so to another.

Come with me to the granule of phlegms.
What seems simultaneous to one will not seem so to another.


In my city at night you are like a mist
and your form and colour the way I excite them.
A burnt umber faucet refreshes.
