Meeting Your Moldy Banana


Realized naked stars in the sky realized profound ream of paper deep brown
cubicles of wasteland,
transluscent burnt umber seams above a crooked foliage.
What is this image but a memory protested of its sweetness?
A irreducable rug making a indespensable thing of a impossible meeting with a sailor.
Everything shifty with equinoctial voices, the salt of the phalanx of umbrellas
and piles of silent bread next to holiday.
