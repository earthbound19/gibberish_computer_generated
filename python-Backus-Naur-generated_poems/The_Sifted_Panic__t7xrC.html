The Sifted Panic


The tremulous grace is vertical on your eye.
And you drowned in the thoughtless and dedicated a degrading lineage.
The dew magnifies in playing your fingernails.

There ought to be a faith of merchants of a nocturnal pile of books fashioning in a room.
To seek another land neither essence nor friendship nor ultraviolet
nor cinnamon but silvery.
A poetic rain of writings.


The child smiles at the gentleman
but the elder does
not smile
when he looks at the crab woman
and the rotten ocean.
A loaf of bread baked with ghostly success and salt.
