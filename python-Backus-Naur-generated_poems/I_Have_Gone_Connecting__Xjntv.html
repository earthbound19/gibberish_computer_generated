I Have Gone Connecting


I could weave
cadaver, bone, and malapertness of pedlars
from shadess of sunburst orange and spheres
with a dull shades of cinnamon aspen
with abberations in my fingernails.
You are the mango of my rambunctious hips.
A red and skeleton marine lake is petrified in the boulevard.
For a day, maybe sextillion,
I rested under a trogle of snakes
at a bio-engineering laboratory, waiting for the mountaineer to be in.
