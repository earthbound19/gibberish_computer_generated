In the face of so many eternities to animosity. In my university at afternoon you are like a troupe of dancers
and your form and colour the way I fashion them.
Which is a iridescent branch of directions
handful or too many to count, chirped
on a board of directors or in the eloquent retinue of players
directions of the fingernails, a calculation in your hearts.

The son smiles at the custodian
but the man does not smile
when he looks at the toucan mother
and the bleak ocean.
My resplendent shoulder seeks you always.
