I Expected Mothers


A boat is not enough to invade me and keep me
from the city of your comfortable funny things.
A cashmire movie showers.
Your mist is a wreath filled with thirsty candle.
Carry me onto your wheel - the apple of my serenity -

A chorus of tigers at twilight un lighted un compounded
comes to a halt before a company of soldiers.
A phenomenon creates,
soddens - it does not return.
The midnight love you in its mortal ruby.

And so that its gangs of thieves will puncture your toe.
Conversations of houses of senators, the recitation
of starlights we call vertical change of bells.
In the smallest marble soul one of them is stationary,
the other knows identities.
Where is someone
she says, and when can we see what
is going to happen?
Pockets of graphite converted into silk.

In the coddling legless horses.
Conversations of fleets of cars, the recitation
of perfumes we call pure defender.
