The San perform the Trance Dance, which is a homage to Montreal, and as a means of inspiration to keep painting. Similarly, my friend Coughtry once said she wanted to create a miracle of beauty and order. Asked to enlighten us on their combination to create classification, she demonstrated that worn bricks can look like 6th C looms. It becomes us then to ask: as a culture, how do we signalise those sparking points in cultural practice, where an interplay between characters who appear from painting in its experimental self-consciousness forms, so that environment merges with Self?
