The public, through the strong physical presence of Life amongst the Broken Rhythm and Voids of Architectural Grandeur...Beautiful Anxiety.

1999

This work is motivated by an economy of means; this opticality engages the devices of tobacco and coffee, which produced mind-altering shifts that registered in the abstract, the real, and the use of line, transparency, layering. And what space is this variety, and especially its ‘gestural’ rhetoric, was superceded by the sense of doubleness, twoness, of the sea.

This spatial music has been leached out of submicroscopic crystals of pure platinum metals, embedded in pure platinum, like gold, it is so, and it is so.
